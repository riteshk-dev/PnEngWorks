About PN Engineering Works

Manufacturing Excellence Since 1999

Founded in Ludhiana, Punjab — India's industrial heartland — PN Engineering Works has grown into a trusted global supplier of precision sewing machine parts. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility houses CNC machining centers, automated assembly lines, and a rigorous quality assurance lab.

  • 40,000+ sq. ft. manufacturing facility in Ludhiana, India
  • 200+ skilled engineers, machinists and quality inspectors
  • In-house R&D for custom part development and OEM support
  • Advanced CMM inspection and salt spray testing equipment
  • Compatibility with Brother, Juki, Singer, Pfaff, Siruba, Jack & more
